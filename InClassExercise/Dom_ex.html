Activities Available at the University of Minnesota

There are a multitude of things to do here at the University of Minnesota. You can attend sporting events, concerts, exercise, join a club, get a work-study job, or just chill with your friends - and those are just a few possibilites

Entertaining, but under the radar activities

  • Attend a Gopher track meet
  • Go to a Gopher baseball game
  • Play ultimate frisbee on the quad

That is my list - yours may be quite different. But, the point is, you should get involved in an activity of your choice, research shows that it will help your grades and enhance your college experience
